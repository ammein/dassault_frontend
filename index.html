Over approximately 1,000 years of its existence, Chess was known as a military strategic tool, An allegory for human affairs, and a measure of genius While the earliest records of chess were in the 7th century, Legend tells us that the game’s origin dates back a century before that. Supposedly, when the youngest prince of the Gupta Empire was killed in battle, His brother devised a way to represent the scene to their grieving mother.
